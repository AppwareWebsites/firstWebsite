Our product

Idavis development board

Idavis development board is an prototyping board to facilitate experimentation, prototype & build electronic devices based on microcontroller, sensors, wireless communication, input/output devices (sensory inputs, led, motor, relay, etc). It is made for learning purposes and can also be used in industrial applications as well. It contains everything needed to support the microcontroller; simply connect it to a computer with a USB cable or power it with a AC-to-DC adapter or battery to get started
